# Business Overview - Dorak

**Dorak** is more than just a queue system; it is a comprehensive Business Growth Platform focused on optimizing the customer service journey.

---

## 🎯 The Vision
To become the leading queue management solution in the Middle East, empowering businesses to provide "Zero-Wait" experiences and improving operational efficiency through data-driven insights.

---

## 💡 The Problem & Solution

### The Problem
- **Customer Frustration**: Long physical lines lead to walk-aways and bad reviews.
- **Staff Burnout**: Managing chaotic crowds is stressful and prone to error.
- **Lack of Data**: Businesses don't know their peak hours or average service times.

### The Solution (Dorak)
- **Virtual Queuing**: Join from anywhere via QR code.
- **Staff Dashboard**: Intuitive interface to manage flow efficiently.
- **Analytics**: Deep insights into business performance.

---

## 💰 SaaS Model (Monetization)

Dorak operates on a tiered subscription model:

| Plan | Target | Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | Micro-businesses | 1 Queue, Basic Analytics |
| **Starter** | Small Shops | 3 Queues, Staff Management |
| **Professional** | Growing Businesses | Unlimited Queues, Real-time SMS, Advanced Analytics |
| **Enterprise** | Large Chains | Multi-branch, White-label, Dedicated Support |
