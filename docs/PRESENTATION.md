# Project Presentation - Dorak

Welcome to the future of Queue Management. Dorak is designed to transform the way businesses interact with their customers.

---

## 📽️ System Showcase

### 🖥️ Professional Dashboard
Manage your business with ease. Our dashboard provides real-time visibility into all active queues, staff performance, and customer satisfaction metrics.

### 📱 Customer Mobile Experience
No app download required! Customers scan a QR code and track their position in real-time through a beautiful, responsive web interface.

### 📊 Real-time Analytics
Visualize your peak hours, average wait times, and staff efficiency. Make data-driven decisions to grow your business.
